Not to be used alone</p> <ul> <li>= equals</li> <li>~= begins with - =~ ends with</li> <li>~=~ contains</li> <li>&lt; smaller then</li> <li>&lt;= equal or smaller then</li> <li>&gt; bigger then</li> <li>&gt;= equal or bigger then</li> <li>&gt;&lt; in list.
